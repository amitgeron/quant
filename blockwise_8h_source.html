72 /* This function manages XORing an input stream with a keystream
73  * to produce an output stream. The keystream is produced in blocks
74  * (ala a block cipher in counter mode).
75  *
76  * partial is the keystream buffer (maintained by the caller)
77  * on entry, *npartial is the currently valid count of bytes in partial:
78  * unused bytes are at the *end*. So *npartial = 4 means the last four
79  * bytes of partial are usable as keystream.
80  * on exit, npartial is updated to reflect the new state of partial.
81  * nblock is the blocksize to accumulate -- partial must be at least
82  * this long!
